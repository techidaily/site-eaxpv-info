## Top 10 Best Disney Christmas Movies of All Time

**1\. A Christmas Carol (2009)**

From Walt Disney Pictures comes the magical retelling of Charles Dickens' classic novel about a heartwarming adventure for the whole family, courtesy of several mysterious Christmas apparitions. Directed by Robert Zemeckis, the 3D motion-capture extravaganza is probably the most substantial yet [classic Christmas movie](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) by Disney made to celebrate Xmas in 2009\. 

**2\. Frozen (2013)**

When it comes to Frozen, it cannot be too suitable for Disney Christmas movies. Went into general theatrical release on November 27, 2013, Frozen is destined to be a blockbuster in holiday season and maybe all through your lifetime, thanks to its great animation and musical technology, grossing over $1.2 billion in worldwide box office revenue and winning two [Academy Awards](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) for Best Animated Feature and Best Original Song ("Let It Go").

**3\. Big Hero 6 (2014)**

Another must-see Disney movie for kids and family to watch on Xmas is the 2014 sensatinal Big Hero 6, which tells us a story about a special bond, deriving from plus-sized inflatable robot Baymax, and prodigy Hiro Hamada, who join hands with a gaggle of friends to form a band of high-tech heroes. This 2014 blockbuster from Disney sets the world on fire, with $185,611,522 box office in USA and wins 8.3 high ratings on IMDB. If you miss this best animated movie from Disney, you can catch up on December 25.

**4\. Mickey's Once Upon a Christmas (1999)**

A classic Christmas movie made by Walt Disney in 1999 split into three segments, with part 1 'Donald Duck Stuck on Christmas' about a retelling of Groundhog Day (1993), with Huey, Dewey, and Louie reliving Christmas ad infinitum, part 2 'A Very Goofy Christmas' about Goofy who tries to overcome his son's skepticism about Santa Claus, and part 3 'Mickey & Minnie's Gift of The Magic' recreating the classic theme of selflessness over the holiday period. This Christmas Disney movies is destined to be part of your holiday tradition, adding sparkle and heart to all your holiday yet to come. 

**5\. Beauty and the Beast: The Enchanted Christmas (1997)**

One of the [top ten Disney movies](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) and a science fiction film, starring Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt. In a world where technology exists to enter the human mind through dream invasion, a highly skilled thief is given a final chance at redemption which involves executing his toughest job till date, Inception. And the ending was open to your own feelings or interpretation.

**6\. The Nightmare Before Christmas (1993)**

Jack Skellington, who hungers for something more than the same old tricks and treats, stumbles upon Christmas town and promptly decides to make the Yuletide his own. Whether you find the Disney movie suited for viewing around Christmas or Halloween, it is still a perfect Disney [Christmas movie for family and kids](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) to enjoy on December 25.

**7\. Ernest Saves Christmas (1988)**

Actually, this is the third film to feature the character Ernest P. Worrell, and chronicles Ernest's attempt to find a replacement for an aging Santa Claus. This is also one that cannot miss from the list of Disney's best Christmas movies. 

**8\. The Search for Santa Paws (2010)**

Magic dogs and an elf team up with two children to rescue Santa who has lost his memory. In the tradition of Disney's classic holiday tales comes a heartwarming movie about the power of giving and the true meaning of Xmas. 

**9\. I'll be home for Christmas (1997)**

A college student experiences difficulty in getting home for Christmas after being hazed by his friends. While struggling to get home in time for Christmas, he learns quite a bit about himself and the true meaning of the holiday. Great holiday movie to watch over and over with your family. 

**10\. The Santa Clause (1994)** 

The fantasy family comedy film stars Tim Allen as Scott Calvin, a divorced father who accidentally causes Santa Claus to fall from his roof on Xmas Eve and slips into the role of Father Christmas. This classic Disney movie is great for Christmas entertainment.
