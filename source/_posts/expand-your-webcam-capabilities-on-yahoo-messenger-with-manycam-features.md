## ManyCam for Yahoo Messenger

Search 

In Yahoo Messenger, open your "Preferences" (Main menu - Messenger - Preferences or open the My Webcam window and click on the Main menu - File - Preferences), click on the "Video&Voice" option and select "ManyCam Virtual Webcam" or "ManyCam Video Source" in the "Camera" dropdown list.
