### More about Disney Movie

**What's Digital Rights Management:** Abbreviated to DRM, it is a general term containing any access control technologies used by copyright holders to protect copyrighted content against piracy and unauthorized copying. DRM simulation can be chaotic. Apple pushes its FairPlay; Microsoft has its Windows DRM; Sony has OpenMG; while **Disney applies X-project protection to its DVD discs** to safeguard itself from piracy. The upshot is that many common DVD rippers cannot work on these Disney DVDs, or those Disney protected DVD movies after ripping turn out to have extremely garbled video and audio.
