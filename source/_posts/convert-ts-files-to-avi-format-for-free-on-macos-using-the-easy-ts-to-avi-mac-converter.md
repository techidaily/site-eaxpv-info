**TS**, abbreviation of MPEG transport stream (MPEG-TS, MTS or TS), is a standard format for transmission and storage of audio, video and Program and System Information Protocol (PSIP) data. It had been originally designed for broadcast such as DVB, ATSC and IPTV. Later it was widely adapted for usage with digital video cameras, recorders and players. Therefore, it's a common thing that you could gain and accumulate a wealth of HD videos in TS file formats downloaded from the Internet or captured by your Canon, Sony or Panasonic HD camcorder.
