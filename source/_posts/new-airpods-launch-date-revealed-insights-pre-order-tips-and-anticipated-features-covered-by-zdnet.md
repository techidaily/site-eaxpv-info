## How to preorder the new AirPods

The [AirPods 4](https://apple.sjv.io/c/159047/435031/7613?&sharedid=zdnet&partnerpropertyid=1980086&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fshop%2Fbuy-airpods%2Fairpods-4&subId1=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp) and new [AirPods Max](https://apple.sjv.io/c/159047/435031/7613?&sharedid=zdnet&partnerpropertyid=1980086&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fshop%2Fbuy-airpods%2Fairpods-max&subId1=zd-%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F-dtp) are available for preorder now on Apple's website, and will officially go on sale on September 20\. 

The AirPods 4 cost $129 for the base version and $179 for a version with active noise cancellation. Other features include an H2 chip, personalized spatial audio, and machine learning-powered voice isolation to remove background noise. 

The AirPods Max will come in new colors (Midnight, Blue, Purple, Orange, and Starlight), and include USB-C charging and iOS 18 features for improved audio features. The price remains the same, at $549\. 

[AirPods Pro 2](https://buy.geni.us/Proxy.ashx?TSID=368250&GR%5FURL=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.amazon.com%2FApple-Generation-Cancelling-Transparency-Personalized%2Fdp%2FB0D1XD1ZV3%2F%3Ftag%3Dzd-buy-button-20%26ascsubtag%3D%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F%7Cc6c3d748-fb91-4cbc-bb39-fa91a44e27a8%7Cdtp&dtb=1) \-- which were released back in 2022 -- will get a software update that includes a hearing protection feature, to keep the volume from damaging your hearing. You'll also find a hearing test that you can take on your iPhone using your AirPods. And perhaps most importantly, the AirPods Pro 2 will get a hearing aid feature that basically allows the AirPods to function as a hearing aid and amplify sounds around you. You can [buy AirPods Pro 2 on sale for $199 at Amazon](https://buy.geni.us/Proxy.ashx?TSID=368250&GR%5FURL=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.amazon.com%2FApple-Generation-Cancelling-Transparency-Personalized%2Fdp%2FB0D1XD1ZV3%2F%3Ftag%3Dzd-buy-button-20%26ascsubtag%3D%5F%5FCOM%5FCLICK%5FID%5F%5F%7Cc6c3d748-fb91-4cbc-bb39-fa91a44e27a8%7Cdtp&dtb=1) right now.
