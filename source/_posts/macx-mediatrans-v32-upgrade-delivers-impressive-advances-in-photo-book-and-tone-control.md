## MacX MediaTrans v3.2 Upgrade Delivers Impressive Advances in Photo, Book & Tone Control

## MacX MediaTrans V3.2 Updated with Huge Achievement on Photo, Book and Ringtone Management 

Nov 4, 2016

![MacX MediaTrans updates](https://www.macxdvd.com/press-room/image/mt-upgrade-1104.jpg) 

_Following the successful worldwide launch of the award-winning iPhone transfer software MacX MediaTrans, today, MacXDVD Software announced a major upgrade to the media transfer solution on iPhone iPad iPod. The latest version 3.2 marks the first milestone update, which expands its support list to ringtone, Voice Memos, iTunes U and Podcast, and makes perfect compatibility with Apple's new iPhone 7 and iOS 10, transforming from the off-the-shelf photo, video and music transfer tool to an all-round iPhone iPad manager._ 

MacXDVD, a worldwide leading provider of desktop and mobile multimedia solutions, announced today the availability of the newest [MacX MediaTrans](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) V3.2, the most sweeping upgrade to its award-winning drag-and-drop iPhone file manager. The new release includes the addition of support for iPhone ringtone, iBook, Voicememeo, iTunes U, Podcast, completely revamped photo and music management and expanded support for iPhone 7/7 Plus/SE, iPad Pro/mini 4/Air 2, and all other Apple devices powered by iOS 10 and earlier.

MacX MediaTrans V3.2 provides an overall transfer solution that gives full control over all contents on iDevices, feel free to get the latest version at [https: //www.macxdvd.com/mediatrans/](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/). 

With the central upgrade focused on expanding the supported media types, a slew of new options have been added in the release, including  
 \- An audio & voice management feature which allows users to import, export and delete iPhone ringtone, Voicememeo, iTunes U, Podcast;   
 \- An [iPhone ringtone maker](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) to snip and clip any music segment from favorite songs and sync it into iPhone as ringtone in one click;   
 \- A book manager, with which users can add or remove books in Epub, PDF and Audiobook, and export books to computers and auto convert Epub into PDF, Html and TXT. 

Beyond the increased key features, important improvements have been made to the underlying technologies required for easy and comprehensive iPhone file management. The significant enhancements include:   
\- Improved music manager that further extends users' ability to transfer music in any formats by adding the capability to convert any incompatible audio formats into MP3 and AAC losslessly;  
\- Upgraded photo management function which allows customizable photo albums creating and deleting, as well as addition, removal, export and import of photos, pictures, images and wallpapers in batches in a flash.

These enhancements extend the scope and power of the [iOS file manager](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) and strip out the complexity to transfer media between iOS and computer, providing customers with greater flexibility, minimum effort and decreased time when it comes to managing and controlling files and storage on iPhone iPad. Plus, the latest version of the iPhone file manager is now built on Apple's latest desktop OS macOS Sierra with full iOS 10 compatibility to support the latest iPhone 7/7 Plus. 

"We have devoted large amount of efforts and time for this upgrade to deliver the ability to freely move data and free up more space on device to fit today's mobile lifestyle." Marked Jack Han, CEO of MacXDVD, "This upgrade is regarded as a milestone in our capacity to swiftly respond to the market trends and our customer's needs."
