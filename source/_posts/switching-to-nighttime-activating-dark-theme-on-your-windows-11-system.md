* Enhance the dark mode experience by changing your PC's theme to a darker one under the "Select a Theme to Apply" section in Personalization.

 Want to give your eyes a break? Windows 11 makes it easy to turn all your onscreen items darkish with its [dark mode](https://youtube-webster.techidaily.com/ed-unraveling-youtubes-monetization-shifts-for-2024/). We'll show you how to enable and disable this mode on your Windows 11 PC.

##  Enable Dark Mode on Windows 11

 In Windows 11, you can activate dark mode by toggling on the option in the Settings app.

 To do that, first, open the [Settings app](https://easy-unlock-android.techidaily.com/in-2024-how-to-show-wi-fi-password-on-oneplus-nord-n30-se-by-drfone-android/) on your PC. Do this by pressing the Windows+i keys at the same time.

 On the Settings screen, from the sidebar to the left, select "Personalization."

![Select "Personalization" in Settings on Windows 11.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/1-settings-personalization.png)

##  Disable Dark Mode on Windows 11

 To disable dark mode and go back to light mode, head into Settings > Personalization > Colors. Then, click the "Choose Your Mode" drop-down menu and select "Light."

![Select "Light" in Settings on Windows 11.](https://static1.howtogeekimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/7-light-mode-windows-11.png)
