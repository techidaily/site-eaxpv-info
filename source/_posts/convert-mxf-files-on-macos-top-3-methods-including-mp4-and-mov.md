## FAQ about MXF Conversion

### 1\. What is MXF format?

 MXF (Material Exchange Format) is a complicated container format for professional digital video and audio media defined by set of SMPTE standards. The file extension of MXF file is ".mxf". MXF format was designed to address a number of problems with non-professional formats originally. 

### 2\. What devices adopt MXF for video encoding? 

 MXF is popular among camcorders from Panasonic, Canon Cinema EOS, Sony XDCAM camcorders, and many other cameras. What's more important, advanced new formats like Sony RAW, CinemaDNG, and XAVC that are used to store 4K and large videos use MXF as an output format.

### 3\. How do I open MXF files?

 While MXF is widely used by video recorders, cameras, digital editing equipment and servers, it's not as friendly as MP4 or MOV. Because the video and audio are stored separately in different file folders. All popular NLE systems can open MXF, including AVID Media Composer, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Apple Final Cut Pro. But if you want to play MXF on Mac, general video players or mobile devices, download a Mac MXF video converter from the above recommendations to freely change MXF format.
