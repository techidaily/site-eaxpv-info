PDF is the most commonly used eBook format as it is supported by almost all eBook readers, tablets, and smartphones. However, the PDF is designed to reproduce fixed-layout pages, which means the PDF format is fixed-format and cannot be reflowed. If you intend to print eBooks, the PDF format is a good choice. However, if you plan to read a PDF file on a device with a screen size less than 10 inches, the reading experience will be horrible. If you have ever read a PDF on Kindle, you must know my feelings—scrolling and zooming in and out to read each line. I bet some of you are still suffering from the bad reading experience now, as Kindle is the most popular eReader. Why not convert PDF to Mobi for better reading on Kindle? Mobi, a native Kindle eBook format, is compatible with Kindle devices very well. In this article, I will introduce you to the 3 best online PDF to Mobi converters and top 3 downloadable PDF to Mobi converters .

Best Free PDF to Mobi Converter Online Convert DRM-free PDF to Mobi format

For those who do not intend to download and install new software on their computers, I would like to recommend some online PDF to Mobi converters to you. I know there are many free PDF to Mobi converters online, but you can never tell which one is the best if you do not use them one by one. From my own tests, I would like to recommend the following three best PDF to Mobi converters. One thing I want to point out is that these online PDF to Mobi converters are completely free.

1: Online PDF to Mobi Converter–PDF2MOBI

PDF2MOBI online converter focuses on converting PDF to other format or any other format to PDF.

How to convert PDF to Mobi with PDF2MOBI?

You can drag and drop your PDF file to the interface of the webpage or just click on “upload files” button to select the file to upload your original PDF file. When the file has been uploaded successfully, it will start to convert automatically. You can download the converted mobi file one by one or download all at a time.

For converting PDF to mobi online, PDF2MOBI is my first choice as it is a native PDF converter which pay its all attention to PDF format. What’s more, it supports batch conversion which is really time-saving.

2: Free Online PDF to Mobi Converter – Convertio.co

Different from PDF2MOBI, Convertio.co supports multiple format conversion, including eBooks, images, videos, audios and so on.

How to convert PDF to Mobi with Convertio.co?

Select the PDF file from your computer, Dropbox, GoogleDrive or URL, then click on “ Convert” button. The PDF file will be uploaded and converted automatically. When the conversion has finished, just click “Download” button to download the converted mobi files.

This online converter can quickly and easily convert PDF to Mobi. It allows users to upload files from different resources, like Google Drive, Dropbox or URL. What’s more, if you do not want to download the converted file to your PC, you can store them on your Google Drive or Dropbox.

3: Free Online PDF to Mobi Converter – Zamzar

As a free online file conversion service provider, Zamzar claims it supports more than 1200 file formats. The interface of this website is neat and well featured. You can easily find the conversion function you want.

How to convert PDF to Mobi with Zamzar?

Go to the Zamzar PDF to Mobi Conversion page, and you will see they have step to step instructions on the website. What you have to do is following the instructions, upload the files, select the output format, fill in your email address and then click on “Convert” Button. Then wait for the email from Zamzar and download the converted Mobi file by clicking the download link they sent.

This is an easy-to-use online PDF to Mobi converter which supports versatile input and output formats. But it is really uncomfortable when it requires me to fill in my email address. If I do not put in my email address, I cannot go to the next stop. It seems that they are collecting users’ information by this method. Although you can upload several files at a time, you still have to download them separately.

Although there are so many free PDF to Mobi converters online, you should consider about the privacy issues before using their services cause you never know what they will do to the PDF file you’ve uploaded to their servers. That’s why I want to introduce the following PDF to Mobi Converter freeware.
